About

A bit about me.

I'm a Computer Science & Electrical Engineering student with a passion for building things end-to-end — from PCB design to production deployments.

I enjoy working at the intersection of hardware and software: writing firmware for microcontrollers, designing circuits, and building the web interfaces that talk to them.

When I'm not coding, you'll find me at the gym, tinkering with electronics, or deep in a rabbit hole on something new.
